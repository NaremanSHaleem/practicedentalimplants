Dr Bashar Al-Naher is the Principal Dentist of The Care Dental Group, award-winning dental clinics (Care Dental Platinum & The Care Dental Practice) in Hammersmith, London and has over 35 years’ experience in General, Cosmetic Dentistry and treatment of nervous patients.

Dr Bashar invented the Enjoyable Dentistry Technique™ (EDT), a technique whereby nitrous oxide and oxygen, along with relaxation instructions are given to each patient throughout dental treatment. He has trained and shared his knowledge and passion with his clinical team at Care Dental Platinum so that more patients can benefit from this unique and award-winning technique.

Dr Bashar is the clinic's Senior Implatologist with a special interest in Dental Implants and Crowns. He works with a team of highly trained dentists who aid him in delivering dental implant treatment, these experienced dentists include Dr Ming-Wei Tang and Dr Safa Al-Naher.

With over 100 Google 5* verified reviews, 3 awards for Best Care and Treatment of Nervous Patients in the whole of the UK, Dr Bashar and his team are confident and proud in not only delivering the top dental results but also making sure that every patient has a relaxing and pain-free experience.
